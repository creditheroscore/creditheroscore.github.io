Lowe's Credit Card for Summer BBQ Essentials

  • Home
  • Blog
  • -Blog Details

Summer is here, and that means it’s time to fire up the grill, invite friends and family over, and enjoy the smoky, savory flavors of a backyard BBQ. But hosting the perfect cookout isn’t just about great recipes—it’s also about having the right tools, appliances, and ingredients. That’s where the Lowe’s Credit Card comes in. With exclusive financing options, cashback rewards, and discounts, it’s the ultimate companion for stocking up on summer BBQ essentials while staying budget-savvy.

Why a Lowe’s Credit Card is a Must-Have for BBQ Season

Grilling season can get expensive fast—especially with rising food costs and supply chain disruptions affecting everything from charcoal to patio furniture. The Lowe’s Credit Card helps offset these expenses with perks like:

  • 6 months of special financing on purchases over $299
  • 5% off everyday purchases (or deferred interest options)
  • Exclusive member discounts on grills, outdoor furniture, and more

Whether you’re upgrading your old grill, building a new outdoor kitchen, or just stocking up on propane and spices, this card ensures you get the most bang for your buck.

Must-Have BBQ Essentials You Can Buy at Lowe’s

High-Quality Grills & Smokers

No BBQ is complete without a reliable grill. Lowe’s carries top brands like Weber, Traeger, and Char-Broil, offering everything from portable gas grills to high-end pellet smokers.

  • Gas Grills: Perfect for quick, even cooking (look for models with side burners for sauces).
  • Pellet Grills: Ideal for low-and-slow smoking (Traeger’s Wi-Fi-enabled models let you monitor temps remotely).
  • Charcoal Grills: For that authentic smoky flavor (the Weber Master-Touch is a classic).

Outdoor Cooking Accessories

A great grill is just the start. Don’t forget:

  • Meat Thermometers (avoid undercooked disasters)
  • Grill Brushes & Cleaners (maintenance is key)
  • Smoker Chips & Pellets (hickory, mesquite, applewood)

Patio & Entertainment Upgrades

Your BBQ isn’t just about food—it’s about the experience. Lowe’s has everything to elevate your outdoor space:

  • Durable Patio Furniture (sectionals, dining sets, fire pits)
  • Outdoor Lighting (string lights, lanterns)
  • Coolers & Beverage Stations (keep drinks ice-cold)

How Inflation & Supply Chain Issues Affect BBQ Planning

Global supply chain delays and inflation have made some BBQ staples pricier or harder to find. Here’s how to adapt:

Meat Prices & Alternatives

Beef prices have surged due to droughts and feed costs. Consider:
- Chicken thighs (juicier and cheaper than breasts)
- Plant-based burgers (Beyond Meat and Impossible options)
- Local butchers (sometimes better deals than supermarkets)

Propane & Charcoal Shortages

Stock up early—shortages happen. The Lowe’s Credit Card helps you buy in bulk during sales.

Sustainable BBQ Practices

With climate change impacting food production, eco-friendly grilling matters:
- Natural lump charcoal (fewer additives than briquettes)
- Reusable grill mats (reduce waste)
- Solar-powered patio lights (cut energy costs)

Maximizing Your Lowe’s Credit Card for Summer Savings

Timing Your Purchases

Lowe’s often runs seasonal sales around Memorial Day, July 4th, and Labor Day. Pair these with your card’s 5% discount for maximum savings.

Using Special Financing

Big-ticket items like grills or patio sets can be financed interest-free for 6 months (on purchases over $299).

Earning Rewards

Every purchase earns rewards—redeem them later for more BBQ supplies or home upgrades.

Final Grill Tips for a Perfect Summer

  • Marinate in advance (saves time and boosts flavor)
  • Prep sides early (pasta salads, grilled veggies)
  • Keep a spray bottle handy (for flare-ups)

With the Lowe’s Credit Card, you’re not just buying a grill—you’re investing in summers full of good food, great company, and unforgettable memories. Fire it up!
